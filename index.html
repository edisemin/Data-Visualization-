Data source: Nextstrain

Data processing: The original Nextstrain ncov open global metadata contains each submitted strain's metadata. To process the data into a format that can be used to visualize some of the clade monthly metrics, all strains are grouped by clade membership then by month. Three metrics 1. monthly clade average S1 mutations, 2. monthly clade average mutational fitness, and 3. monthly clade frequencies are computed from the nested grouped data.
